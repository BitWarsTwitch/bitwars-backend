Introduction

Welcome to BitWars, a Twitch Extension designed to enhance your streaming experience through interactive animations triggered by bit purchases. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to being transparent about what data we access, why we access it, and how we handle it. By using BitWars, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect minimal data to operate the BitWars Extension. Here is a summary of what we access and why:

Twitch Username: We access your Twitch username after a successful purchase of bits within the Extension. This information is used solely to display your name within the on-screen animation.

Note: This data is not stored or retained by BitWars or any third-party server; it is accessed solely in real-time for immediate display within the Extension.

2. Backend Data Storage

Game States: BitWars maintains game states on our backend to facilitate gameplay flow. This does not include any user-specific data or personal information.

The backend data storage is limited strictly to the internal states necessary for the Extension to function as intended.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data with third parties. BitWars is a standalone extension and does not transmit user information beyond the Twitch platform.

4. Security Measures

While BitWars does not store personal information, we employ best practices to secure our backend infrastructure, ensuring that all gameplay data is processed safely and efficiently.
